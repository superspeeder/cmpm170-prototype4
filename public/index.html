How To Play:

Click and hold to move your bird towards your cursor! Beware, you have limited movement per turn.

End your turn in water (blue tiles) to collect it, don't run out of water or you will die!

Click on enemy birds to attack when within range!

End your turn on territory (gray tiles) to conquer more land (expand your territory)! Warning: enemy birds will steal your territory.

Win the game by taking over the entire map!
